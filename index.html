Bio

My research interests center around solving spatial-temporal data mining problems and applying the developed learning approaches to broad AI applications in time series forecasting, sentiment analysis in NLP, and healthcare outcome prediction.

I am a Ph.D. student in the Wayne State University, mentored by Dongxiao Zhu. I received my M.E. degree from University of Chinese Academy of Sciences, and B.E. degree from Nanjing University of Science and Technology.
