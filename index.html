Bio

I am highly motivated to pursue a career in the industry relating to the application of machine learning (ML) and deep learning (DL). My research interests center around developing ML and DL approaches to address some social good related problems, such as medical image analysis (BIBM-20) and electric vehicle charging recommendations (ECML-22). I have also done projects relating to the interpretability, robustness, and fairness of DL-based models (IJCAI-22).

I am a Ph.D. student in the Wayne State University, mentored by Dongxiao Zhu. I received my M.E. degree from University of Chinese Academy of Sciences, and B.E. degree from Nanjing University of Science and Technology.
